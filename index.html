About Us

The Figure Skating Boutique operates in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA), and has a broad reach in the area. The Figure Skating Boutique is a retail skating boutique that was established in 1972, and has been in the same location for the past 25 plus years. It is well known in the skating community as a quality boutique supplying the latest in skating apparel and accessories, boots, blades and services such as sharpening, mounting and leather care.

Figure Skating Boutiques involvement in the Central Ontario Section has allowed for it to work with the skating community in the greater Toronto area. With this relationship The Figure Skating Boutique has garnered a reputation for its ability to offer end to end skating services on site.

We carry Riedell, Jackson, Gam, Graf, Risport, Harlick, Edea, and Sp Teri. We have a large quantity of SK Skates in stock at clearance prices. Our blades are Wilson, Mitchell and King, MK, Ultima, ISE. We carry Twizzle, Quad Club, Jerry's and Mondor Dresses and Tights. Accessories are Hot Chili's

Figure Skating Boutique has 2 locations, at 6174 Yonge St. in Toronto and 140 Mulock Drive in Newmarket. Located centrally in the GTA, the store is in close proximity to many major training centres. The Toronto store is located on a main transit artery. We are within one kilometre of the subway system and are serviced by both the Viva and GO Bus systems. Four of the five major training centres are located within a 20 minute drive. Our central location makes it beneficial to offer services to all clubs within the GTA.

Our reputation has grown to include international competitive and recreational level skaters from Mexico, Japan, China and Korea, United States as well as our Canadian skaters. They come to the store for their equipment and apparel, our knowledge and our expert ability to fit skates properly has enabled us to be recognised by international skaters and their respective associations.

Regions Serviced:

Figure Skating Boutique offers everything you need for Ice Skating and Figure Skating. From Figure Skating Dresses to Figure Skating Equipment, the Figure Skates and Ice Skates themselves and much more. Our stores are located in Toronto and Newmarket, however our online store services across Canada (Edmonton, Calgary, Regina, Winnipeg, Ottawa, Montreal, Fredericton, Halifax, St. Johns, Whitby, North Bay, Sudbury, London, etc.) The United States (Tampa, Syracuse, San Francisco, San Diego, San Antonio, Sacramento, Pittsburg, Philadelphia, Orlando, Oakland, New York, New Jersey, Miami, Los Angeles, Lake Placid, Jacksonville, Houston, Detroit, Dallas, Chicago, Buffalo, etc.) and the whole world!

Vision Statement:

Customer relationships mean the world to us. The Figure Skating Boutique has become the premier source of skating products and services for competitive and recreational skaters. Our goal is to provide the skater with the right product, at the right time and at the right price to meet their particular requirements based on the skill level that they have reached.

Mission Statement:

Figure Skating Boutique will offer the latest quality product and services to our customers. We will make the comfort of skaters our primary goal. We will put customers first in everything that we do. We will conduct our business with uncompromising integrity. We will trust and respect all individuals. We will act with speed and agility in meeting the expectations of our customers.
